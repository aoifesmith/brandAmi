About

brandAmi services offer access to brand ambassadors who support a range of products and services on an International level. Ami is the French for friend! We connect companies with suitably screened ambassadors to save businesses time and effort. Ambassadors of brandAmi benefit from contracts created through our platform.

Criteria

At brandAmi we aim to reduce time sourcing and aligning the right ambassadors and companies. We provide a screening of eligibility requirements that must be met to be a part of our community at the levels required by our clients.
